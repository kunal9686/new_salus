# Salus: Sanctuary of Reflection

Salus is a private-first mental wellness platform designed to transform raw emotional input into structured psychological growth through a fusion of AI and established therapeutic frameworks.

## Core Features

### 1. Daily Mood Compass
A central dashboard for tracking emotional states, consistency streaks, and visual trends of your "inner weather" over time.
- **Mood Check-ins:** Log daily feelings (Bad, Neutral, Good, Great).
- **Consistency Tracking:** Maintain streaks to build a habit of reflection.
- **Emotional Landscape:** Interactive line charts powered by Recharts.

### 2. The Reflect Hub
Six specialized modules designed to guide deep introspection using proven frameworks:
- **Free Journal:** Unstructured writing for pure expression.
- **Ikigai Discovery:** Find your purpose at the intersection of passion, mission, vocation, and profession.
- **Life Audit:** Visualize balance across 8 key life areas (Mental, Physical, Relationships, etc.) using Radar Charts.
- **Identity Reflection:** Reclaim your narrative and identify core values during transformative times.
- **Stoic Perspective:** Use the "Dichotomy of Control" to manage anxiety and focus on what you can change.
- **CBT Reframing:** Deconstruct automatic negative thoughts and build balanced, realistic alternatives.

### 3. Active Listener (Grow)
An AI-powered growth assistant that utilizes multimodal technology to understand you better.
- **Guided Entry:** Starts sessions with one of 20 psychologically-backed reflection questions.
- **Voice Emotion Detection:** Uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to analyze vocal subtext (pacing, tone, pauses) to detect latent emotions.
- **Growth Insights:** Provides structured feedback and recommends specific Reflect modules based on your state.

### 4. Resilience Gallery (Inspire)
A curated library of 25 powerful true stories of individuals who overcame extreme adversity.
- **Resilience Narratives:** Learn from the lives of Malala Yousafzai, Viktor Frankl, Nelson Mandela, and others.
- **Actionable Lessons:** Each story concludes with a specific reflection prompt to help apply their lessons to your life.

### 5. Personalized Wellness Chatbot
A contextual AI companion that provides tailored support.
- **Personalized Memory:** References your wellness goals and past journal entries to provide relevant advice.
- **Safe Space:** A non-judgmental interface for exploring thoughts and seeking immediate wellness guidance.

### 6. Read Aloud (TTS)
Integrated Text-to-Speech for all AI-generated content.
- **Accessibility:** Listen to AI guidance and resilience stories.
- **Human Connection:** Makes the "Sanctuary" feel more alive and supportive.

## Design Philosophy: "Happy Sanctuary"
The UI is a sophisticated hybrid of **Glassmorphism** (frosted transparency) and **Claymorphism** (soft, pill-shaped depth).
- **Colors:** Peach Blossom, Sunrise Yellow, and Sky Blue on a warm Cream base.
- **Typography:** Elegant Serif (Alegreya) for body and Geometric (Belleza) for headlines.
